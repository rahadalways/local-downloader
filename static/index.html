Help & Feedback

Local Downloader is a standalone media retrieval utility running entirely on your local machine.

Troubleshooting Downloads:

  • Instagram/Facebook blocks: Open settings, select Chrome or Edge in cookies to authenticate.
  • Slow Speeds: Make sure your internet connection is active. Some platforms limit download speed on non-authenticated requests.
  • Invalid Links: Verify that the video is public and accessible without a custom invitation link.

System Dependencies:

Features standard integration with FFmpeg to automatically mux separate audio/video feeds for 1080p and higher qualities.
